SDG 4 The Global Mission

At Personal Learning Hub, our core operations are directly aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 4.

This goal aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all. We believe that education is a fundamental human right, not a privilege reserved for the wealthy.

"By 2030, ensure that all girls and boys get complete free, equitable and quality primary and secondary education leading to relevant and effective learning outcomes. " ~SDG-4, Target - 1
